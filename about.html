About Me!!!

JOHN CARL BORJA LABRADOR

Hey there! I’m John Carl Labrador, 19 years old, proudly from Barangay 32-C La Paz East, Laoag City. Born on December 7, 2005 (yep, a certified December baby — practically a walking Christmas gift). My favorite color is light blue — calm, cool, and maybe because it reminds me of the sky when I’m daydreaming instead of being productive. I spend most of my free time reading novels or watching anime — uh... you can say I’m a mix of “bookworm” and “binge-watcher.” I’m also an animal lover, especially cats. I have one named Miyu, and she’s four months old. Already acts like the queen of the house. I swear, sometimes I think she owns me, not the other way around. Aside from that, I really enjoy cooking. I’m no MasterChef yet, but I can whip up a decent meal — just don’t ask me to bake because even my oven gets nervous when I try. Right now, I’d say I’m still figuring myself out — learning new things, making mistakes, laughing at them, and trying again. Life’s a work in progress, and so am I.
